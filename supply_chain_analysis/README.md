# 📦 Supply Chain Analytics Project | End-to-End Data Analysis & BI Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an **end-to-end Supply Chain Analytics pipeline**, transforming raw logistics and transactional data into actionable business insights. It highlights core competencies in **Data Analysis, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Cleaning, and Business Intelligence (BI)** using Python and Power BI.

Designed as a **portfolio-ready project**, it showcases the ability to solve real-world supply chain challenges such as **delivery delays, profitability optimization, and customer segmentation**.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Analyze supply chain performance and identify inefficiencies
* Detect patterns in **late deliveries and risk factors**
* Evaluate **product performance and profitability trends**
* Build interactive dashboards for **data-driven decision-making**

---

## 💼 Business Impact (Quantified Insights)

* 📉 Identified **high-risk late deliveries**, enabling potential reduction in delivery delays
* 📦 Improved visibility into **shipping performance gaps (actual vs scheduled delivery)**
* 💰 Highlighted **top-performing product categories contributing to revenue growth**
* 🌍 Uncovered **regional sales and profit trends** for strategic decision-making
* 👥 Segmented customers to support **targeted business strategies**

> ⚡ *This project simulates how data analytics can directly support operational efficiency and strategic planning in supply chain management.*

---
